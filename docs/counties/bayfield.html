5  Bayfield

Bayfield County (pop. 16,000) is solidly Democratic, but a divide has emerged between the less populous southern part of the county and the more liberal communities on the “cornucopia,” as locals call the peninsula sticking out into Lake Superior.

The most Democratic-leaning municipalities include the Town of Russell (home of the Red Cliff Band) and the cities of Bayfield and Washburn. The most Republican communities include the rural towns of Namakagon and Cable.
