62  Sheboygan

Sheboygan County (pop. 118,000) is located on Lake Michigan, about halfway between Milwaukee and Green Bay. In most respects, the county is demographically similar to the state as a whole. Politically, the county leans Republican.

62.3 municipality election results

The population center of Sheboygan County is the City of Sheboygan, where about 42% of residents live. There are 21 other communities with populations between 1,000 and 9,000. Sheboygan City leans Democratic while the other municipalities lean Republican.
