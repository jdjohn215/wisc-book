58  Rusk

Rusk County (pop. 14,000) is located in northwestern Wicsonsin. The county is 94% white and has very low population density. Educational attainment is lower than the state average, and the median income is much lower. After a closely competitive series of elections in the 2000s, Republicans have won most recent elections by large margins.

58.3 municipality election results

The two most populous municipalities in Rusk County are the City of Ladysmith (pop. 3,200) and the Town of Flambeau (pop. 1,300). Both of these lean Republican, Flambeau much more so than Ladysmith.
