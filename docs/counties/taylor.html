64  Taylor

Taylor County (pop. 20,000) is located in northern Wisconsin. Its population is 95% white, with median incomes and levels of college attendance that are lower than the state average. The county is among the most Republican in the state.

About 4,300 people live in the City of Medford, while another 2,500 live in the surrounding Medford town. Every other Taylor County municipality has fewer than 1,000 residents. All munciipalities lean substantially Republican.
