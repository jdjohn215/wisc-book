47  Oneida

Oneida County (pop. 38,000) is located in north central Wisconsin. It’s population is 94% white and much older than the state average. Relative to some other northern counties, a somewhat higher share of its population has attended at least some college. The county shifted Republican during the 2010s, but not as strongly as some of its neighbors.

47.3 municipality election results

Oneida’s largest municipalities are the City of Rhinelander (pop. 8,200) and Minocqua town (pop. 5,000), the latter of which is a popular tourist destination. Rhinelander leans Democratic, while the remaining municipalities lean Republican.
