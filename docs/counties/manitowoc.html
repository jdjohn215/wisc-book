37  Manitowoc

Manitowoc County (pop. 81,000) is located south of Green Bay, along the Lake Michigan coastline. The population is 88% white, with generally lower levels of college attendance and household income than the state as a whole. The county has shifted in a Republican direction over the past two decades.

About 35,000 people live in the City of Manitowoc and another 11,000 in the adjacent City of Two Rivers. These two municipalities are the most politically competitive in the county, but Republican candidates usually carry a majority even here.
