11  Calumet

Calumet County (pop. 52,000) is part of the Fox Cities region of Wisconsin, a set of small cities and suburban areas on the shores of Lake Winnebago and south of Green Bay. Calumet County has long been a reliably Republican area, with GOP candidates winning every race for governor since 2000 and all but one presidential and US Senate race, respectively.

11.3 municipality election results

Only small portions of the cities of Appleton and Menasha are located within Calumet County. These city fragments are the most Democratic-leaning areas of the county. The most conservative communites are the Village of Potter and surrounding Town of Rantoul.
