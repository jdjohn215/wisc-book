2  Adams

Adams County (pop. 21,000) is located in central Wisconsin. It has the second highest median age and seventh lowest median household income out of all 72 counties. It has trended distinctly more Republican over the past 20 years. The county voted Democratic in 8 out of 9 elections for president, governor, and US senator during 2000-2010. Since 2010, Republicans have won 12 out of 14 such races.
