72  Waupaca

Waupaca County (pop. 52,000) is located in central Wisconsin about 60 miles west of Green Bay. The county is 93% white and an unusually high share of adults have just a high school degree. The median household income is similar to the state average. Politically, the county leans firmly Republican. Trump improved his 2020 performance relative to 2016.

72.3 municipality election results

Only about 10% of the county lives in the largest municipality, Waupaca city (pop. 6,300). The remainder is spread across numerous other communities; there are 18 with between 1,000 and 5,600 residents. All of these municipalities lean Republican, though the City of Waupaca is the most competitive.
