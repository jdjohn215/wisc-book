46  Oconto

Oconto County (pop. 39,000) is located in notheastern Wisconsin. Its population is 94% white and college attendance rates are below the state average, while incomes are similar. The county has shifted sharply to the right over the past two decades.

46.3 municipality election results

Oconto’s population is distributed among many small communities. The largest two are Little Suamnico town (pop. 5,500) and Oconto city (pop. 4,600). All municipalities lean Republican, although the City of Oconto is the most competitive.
